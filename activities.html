Problem Solving & Life Skills

Extra Curricular Activities

Our extra-curricular program supports holistic development creativity, physical skills, self-regulation and collaboration. Activities are led by experienced instructors using playful, safe and age-appropriate methods.

Creative

Clay & Craft Studio

Our Clay & Craft Studio at Satyam Tiny Tots Bellary gives every child the freedom to touch, feel, mould and create. Every session develops fine motor skills, hand-eye coordination and creative thinking in the most fun, messy and magical way possible

Art

Art & Painting Studio

The Art & Painting Program at Satyam Tiny Tots Bellary is a structured, age-appropriate creative curriculum designed to develop fine motor skills, colour awareness, sensory learning & artistic expression forming a strong foundation for school readiness at Bellary's top-rated preschool.

Sports

Sports & Movement

we believe a child who moves well, learns well. Our outdoor physical education and movement program develops gross motor skills, balance, coordination, stamina and social skills nurturing healthy, active and confident little learners from the very first day.

Swimming

Swimming Basics

We introduce your child to the water gently, safely and joyfully. Our supervised swimming basics program builds water confidence, body coordination, gross motor development and physical fitness. giving every child a life skill that stays with them forever.

Drama

Drama & Roleplay

Our Drama & Play program at Satyam Tiny Tots Bellary develops public speaking confidence, creative expression, emotional intelligence and social-emotional learning giving every child the courage, voice and stage they truly deserve.

Wellness

Kids Yoga

Our Kids Yoga program at Bellary's top pre school develops body flexibility, emotional regulation, mindfulness, gross motor skills and self-awareness nurturing calm, confident and healthy little learners every single day.
