Frequently Asked Questions

Admissions & Enrollment ▾
What are the age criteria for each class? +
We accept children from 2.5 to 5 years across Pre-K, KG1 and KG2. Exact placement is determined during orientation and assessment.
How do I apply for admissions? +
Applications are accepted online or via our admissions desk. Please bring birth certificate, recent photo and immunisation record.
Are there open days or trial classes? +
Yes — we hold monthly open days and limited trial sessions. Contact admissions to reserve a spot.
Daily Routine & Meals ▾
What does a typical day look like? +
Our day includes circle time, guided play, outdoor activity, creative sessions and calm rest times to balance learning and play.
Do you provide meals and snacks? +
We provide healthy snacks and lunch options. Please inform us about allergies or special dietary needs during registration.
How are naps handled? +
Naps are in a supervised, calm space with individual mats. Schedules are adjusted per child's routine and family preferences.
Health & Safety ▾
What safety measures are in place? +
Secure entry, child-safe furniture, trained staff and first-aid-certified caregivers. We perform regular safety checks.
How do you handle illnesses? +
Parents are contacted immediately if a child is unwell. We follow health guidance on exclusions and re-admissions.
Are staff background-checked? +
Yes — all staff undergo background checks and regular professional training to ensure safe, high-quality care.
Learning & Curriculum ▾
What teaching approach do you follow? +
We follow a play-based, inquiry-led approach focusing on social-emotional learning, early literacy and creative expression.
How is progress shared with parents? +
We provide regular progress notes, parent-teacher meetings and practical take-home activities to support learning at home.
Do you support language development? +
Yes — targeted language activities, storytelling and small-group interactions to build vocabulary and expressive skills.
Parent Engagement & Extras ▾
How can parents participate? +
We welcome parent volunteers, workshops and family events. Seasonal celebrations often involve families joining classroom activities.
Are extracurriculars available? +
Optional extras such as music, movement and creative clubs are available depending on age group and schedule.
How are fees and payments handled? +
Fees are billed monthly or termly. We provide clear invoices and multiple payment options; contact admissions for details.
